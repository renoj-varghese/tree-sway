sway freq: 0.45Hz

Tree Sway Frequency is the number of complete circles, or back-and-forth motions, that a tree makes in one second. Frequency is determined by the shape of the trees and does not change with a stronger wind. Trees with a higher frequency are less susceptible to storm damage.

sway amplitude: 0.5M

Tree Sway Amplitude is the distance that a tree bends from its resting position when in motion. Amplitude increases in stronger winds, but not all trees break at the same amplitude. Trees develop different capacities to bend and return to their upright positions depending on their species and the environment in which they were raised.
